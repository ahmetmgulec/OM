# Top 10 UI/UX Improvements for OM

Prioritized by impact and feasibility.

---

## 1. **Date separators in message list**
Add "Today", "Yesterday", or "March 15, 2025" between messages when the date changes. Makes it easier to follow conversations and improves readability.

---

## 2. **Avatars in messages**
Show sender avatars next to each message (or when a new author appears). Improves recognition of who wrote what and visual hierarchy.

---

## 3. **Scroll-to-bottom FAB**
Show a floating "scroll to bottom" button when there are new messages below the viewport. Lets users quickly return to the latest messages after scrolling up.

---

## 4. **Escape key closes modals**
Handle `Escape` to close any open modal and return focus to the trigger. Improves keyboard use and aligns with common expectations.

---

## 5. **Auto-dismiss notifications**
Notifications auto-dismiss after 4–5 seconds, with a visible close (×) button for manual dismiss. Adds slide-in/out transitions for smoother feedback.

---

## 6. **Rename sidebar header**
Change "Select a channel" to something like "Channels" or the server name. Clarifies context when a channel is already selected.

---

## 7. **Message grouping**
Group consecutive messages from the same sender within a time window (e.g. 5 minutes). Collapse repeated headers and only show avatar + name on the first message. Reduces clutter.

---

## 8. **Clickable links in messages**
Parse URLs in message text and render them as links (`target="_blank"`, `rel="noopener noreferrer"`). Improves usability for shared links.

---

## 9. **Improved empty states**
Replace plain text empty states with clearer visuals and CTAs: illustration, short onboarding copy, and prominent action buttons.

---

## 10. **Unread channel badges**
Show an unread count or dot on channels with new messages since last visit. Helps users notice activity and prioritize channels.

---

*Source: UI/UX audit of OM application*
